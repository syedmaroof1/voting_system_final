Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

"Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas, Sabka Vishwas"

About the Party

The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) is one of the two major political parties in India, along with the Indian National Congress. Founded in 1980, the BJP is a right-wing party with close ideological and organizational links to the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS).

Key Principles

  • Integral Humanism: Philosophy of Deendayal Upadhyaya
  • Cultural Nationalism: Promoting Indian culture and values
  • Economic Development: Focus on growth and infrastructure
  • Good Governance: Transparent and efficient administration
  • National Security: Strong defense and internal security

Major Achievements

  • Digital India Initiative
  • Swachh Bharat Abhiyan (Clean India Mission)
  • Make in India Campaign
  • Ayushman Bharat Healthcare Scheme
  • GST Implementation
  • Infrastructure Development Projects

Vision for Future

The BJP envisions a developed India (Viksit Bharat) by 2047, focusing on economic growth, technological advancement, social welfare, and maintaining India's cultural heritage while embracing modernity.
